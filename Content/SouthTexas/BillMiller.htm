Bill Miller Barbecue

General Information

Location: Multiple locations in San Antonio and surrounding areas

Date Established: 1953

Pitmaster: N/A

Website: billmillerbbq.com

Overview

My family moved to San Antonio when I was nine years old. I'm sure I had at least some barbecue before that, but this is the first place I can recall. Even as a kid, though, I remember it being pretty bland. Basically, what made it memorable was how unmemorable it was.

However, my paternal grandfather, who also lived in the area at the time, LOVED this place and always wanted it whenever we had family gatherings. He smoked three packs a day, though, so I'm thinking his taste buds were blown out and not the most discerning.

Ron Recommends

Hmm... I have not tried anything from this place in at least 35 years, so I'm not sure what I can honestly recommend. If I had to go by memory, though, I'd recommend a side of pinto beans. I seem to remember enjoying them. They weren't as good as my mom's but decent enough.

Ron Rates It

When I look at the Bill Miller website, which displays pictures of their food, I don't see a whole lot that looks particularly appetizing. Just saying.

There are many locations throughout San Antonio, so if you are in the area and want to form your own opinion about Bill Miller, you can easily find one.
