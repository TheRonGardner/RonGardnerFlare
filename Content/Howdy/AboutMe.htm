A little bit more about me... I live in Texas, outside of Dallas (duh; it wouldn't make a lot of sense to focus on Texas barbecue if I lived in Vermont). I have a B.S. in Agricultural Journalism from Texas A&M University in College Station (trust me, it's a legit degree program). I've been a technical writer for over 25 years, ever since I graduated from A&M. I currently work as a remote technical writer for an enterprise performance management software company.

I'm married to my wife, Shannon, who actively encourages and shares my barbecue obsession. She's my partner in BBQ crime! Our hobbies include traveling around Texas, watching and talking about movies, and trying new places to eat... with an emphasis on barbecue, of course!

We also have 6 cats that run the house, only one of which seems to like barbecue. Don't worry, I give her only tiny pieces of brisket or turkey from time to time. I watch out for her health!
