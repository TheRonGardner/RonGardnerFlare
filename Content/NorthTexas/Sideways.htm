Sideways

General Information

Location: Rockwall

Date Established: 2017?

Pitmaster: Josh Deaton

Website: sidewaysbbq.com

Overview

The sign on the front of this place says only "Sideways". No mention of barbecue, so go figure. Their website (listed above) indicates it is indeed a barbecue place. I'm just saying I might not have known it had anything to do with barbecue if I strolled by the place without knowing.

As far as my take on the food there... well... they can't all be "winners". I figured I should include at least one mediocre place in this project so I don't come across as a "Barbecue Pollyanna". Unfortunately for Sideways, it met the low bar.

Before visiting, I had really high hopes for this place. People online talked a really big game about it. My meal, however, did not live up to the hype. Maybe it was just an off day for them, I don't know, but my visit didn't endear me to the place. I'll try going back again one day and give them a second chance.

Ron Recommends

The pork spareribs... I guess??? Honestly, I don't really remember a whole lot about this meal.

Ron Rates It

Is "Meh" a rating? It really was middle of the road, so I'll split the 5 stars in half.

This place is located on the shores of Lake Ray Hubbard, so at least the view is kind of nice.
