Overview

Swung by after dropping my wife at the airport. Curious...

Ron's Review

I had the 2-meat plate with the following:

  • Moist brisket

  • Pepper jack sausage

  • Pinto beans

  • Green chile mac and cheese

I saw bacon burnt ends on the menu and absolutely had to try them, so I ordered a half-pound.

Meats

Bacon burnt ends were excellent and the best thing I tried this visit. In fact, I pulled a "car fries" move and snuck a couple on the way home.

Brisket was good, but a large slab of meat. Pepper jack cheese sausage was very good.

Sides

The pinto beans were more like chili beans, but unlike any I ever had before. Large onion pieces that were nice. Green chile mac and cheese was creamy and had a nice peppery note.

Sauce

They didn't give me any. I don't know if that was an oversight, or if they have a Kreuz Market approach to sauce, which is none.

Dessert

I passed on desserts, given the travel time from Love Field to McKinney, where I live. The banana pudding sounded VERY intriguing, and I will try when I return here.

Ron Rates It

This pace is on the "Honorable Mentions" list for Texas Monthly, so I was interested. It was most definitely very good, but not mind-blowing. However, there was some travel time involved until I could enjoy the meal. That being said, I will return here to try in person and update my review.

It's in a pretty busy area near a major airport, so be prepared. It's not a bad place to kill some time if you have some wiggle room before a flight and can get a ride to the airport.
