Pinkerton's Pit Barbecue

General Information

Locations: Houston/San Antonio

Date Established: 2016

Pitmaster: Grant Pinkerton

Website: pinkertonsbarbecue.com

Overview

My wife and I love to vacation on Galveston Island, which is south of Houston and roughly just over a five-hour drive from our house north of Dallas. I don't do well with car trips longer than three hours, so I like to break up trips that way with multiple stops during the journey. We stumbled across some pretty great places because of that, and Pinkerton's was no exception.

In 2018, during a late summer trip to Galveston, I talked my wife into making a stop at Pinkerton's. At the time, I was fixated on visiting as many joints as I could on Texas Monthly Magazine's "Top 50 Barbecue Joints in Texas" list (the 2017 version, not the current list updated in 2025). I think my wife was just humoring me, but as soon as she saw smoked boudin on the menu, she was in.

You order "off the pit" here, which, if you're not careful, means you can end up with way more food than you intend. However, the food looked and smelled too tasty not to try multiple things on the menu, so we loaded up:

No way we could eat all that in one setting. I was glad we brought a cooler to take the leftovers to Galveston with us!

Ron Recommends

Beef ribs were available the day we visited, so I had to go for it, even though I had already ordered moist brisket. Both were delicious and had great smoke rings. The aforementioned smoked boudin link was fantastic and something I look forward to trying again whenever I get back to Pinkerton's.

The sides were also great. We tried the macaroni and cheese, pinto beans, and jalapeno cheese rice. They did not disappoint!

Even as stuffed as we were near the end of the meal, we also tried the banana cake for dessert. It was one of the best barbecue joint desserts I've ever had! Unfortunately, Pinkerton's no longer provides banana cake, but I am very intrigued to try the Bananas Foster Pudding during my next visit.

Ron Rates It

We will definitely return to Pinkerton's SOON!

In addition to its indoor seating, Pinkerton's has several outdoor picnic tables. If you can nab one on a pleasant day, it's not a bad way to spend some time while enjoying some fantastic 'cue.
