Ron's Top 3s

A lot of people ask me "What is your favorite barbecue place/brisket/ribs/etc.?" These questions involve a little more elaboration beyond what can be covered in a simple FAQ.

After much consideration, I created a list of my favorite "Top 3" things from the different places I've visited. These lists are based solely on memory and most likely will change as I visit new or re-visit old places.

Best Barbecue Joints

  1. The Salt Lick (Driftwood)
  2. Hutchins Barbecue (McKinney)
  3. Pinkerton's Texas Pit Barbecue (Houston)

Best Brisket

  1. Burnt ends, The Salt Lick (Driftwood)
  2. Burnt ends, Hutchins Barbecue (McKinney)
  3. Moist brisket, Franklin Barbecue (Austin)

Best Sausage

  1. Jalapeno cheddar pork sausage, Hutchins Barbecue (McKinney)
  2. Smoked crawfish boudin, Bodacious BBQ (Hallsville)
  3. Beef sausage, Louie Mueller Barbecue (Taylor)

Best Pork Ribs

  1. Baby back ribs, The Grist Mill (Gruene)
  2. Baby back ribs, Stanley's Famous Pit Bar-B-Q (Tyler)
  3. Spareribs, Joseph's Riverport Barbecue (Jefferson)

Best Beef Ribs

  1. Pinkerton's Texas Pit Barbecue (Houston)
  2. The Salt Lick (Driftwood)
  3. Bodacious BBQ (Hallsville)

Best Smoked Turkey

  1. Hutchins Barbecue (McKinney)
  2. Joseph's Riverport Barbecue (Jefferson)
  3. Stanley's Famous Pit Bar-B-Q (Tyler)

Best Barbecue Sandwich

  1. The Brother-in-Law, Stanley's Famous Pit Bar-B-Q (Tyler)
  2. The Toddfather, Cattleack Barbecue (Farmer's Branch)
  3. Chopped BBQ sandwich, Luling Bar-B-Q (Luling)

Best Barbecue Specialty Item

  1. Agave glazed bacon burnt ends, Bodacious Bar-B-Q (Hallsville)
  2. Texas Twinkie, Hutchins Barbecue (McKinney)
  3. BBQ Frito pie, Stanley's Famous Pit Bar-B-Q (Tyler)

Best Side Dish

  1. Tater tot casserole, Opie's BBQ (Spicewood)
  2. Brisket pinto beans, Hutchins Barbecue (McKinney)
  3. Elotes, 2M Smokehouse (San Antonio)

Best Dessert

  1. Banana cake*, Pinkerton's Texas Pit Barbecue (Houston)
  2. Blackberry cobbler ala mode, The Salt Lick (Driftwood)
  3. Sweet potato pie, Leon's World's Finest In-and-Out Bar-B-Que (Galveston)

    *Unfortunately, Pinkerton's no longer offers banana cake. However, it does now offer a Bananas Foster bread pudding, which I will definitely try the next time I am there.
