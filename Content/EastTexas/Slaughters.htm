Ron's Reviews

(6/20/25)

We stayed in Longview the previous night and made a morning stop in Simms in Bowie County, so there was a little travel involved with this visit. After our visit to Simms and a pit stop at Duke's in Mount Vernon, my wife and I firmly agreed we HAD to stop by Slaughter's on the way home. We totally made the right choice!

TX Frito Pie with brisket

I don't have the words to explain how freaking good this was. I've had some GREAT bbq Frito pies in the past, but this was the best I've ever had.
